# Anfitrião

## Register
product

## Users
Portuguese AL (Alojamento Local) hosts — individuals and micro property managers with 1 to 10 properties. Mobile-first users, often managing their property alongside a day job. Primary language: Portuguese. Their guests speak EN, ES, FR, DE, PT, IT. The host is time-poor and stressed; every interaction must feel like relief, not friction.

## Product Purpose
A focused daily driver for AL hosts in Portugal. Not a full PMS replacement for sprint 1. Three jobs: see today's arrivals, reply to guest messages in any language using AI, and prepare SEF/SIBA guest filings without manual typing. Replaces the chaos of Booking.com extranet + Airbnb host app + manual SIBA portal spread across 3 devices.

## Brand Voice
Direct, warm, confident Portuguese. No filler words. No corporate English. Feels like a local friend who knows hospitality tech. The interface speaks first — words are a last resort. One idea per sentence.

## Anti-References
- Amenitiz: templated website builder feel, font soup, "Melhor preço garantido" generic copy
- Generic SaaS blue (#3B82F6 everywhere, "inter on white")
- Hotel luxury aesthetic (marble, serif-heavy, gold accents)
- Crypto/fintech neon-on-dark
- Startup cream (gradient purple-to-blue hero, rounded everything)
- Side-stripe borders on any element — never

## Design Principles
- Mobile canonical; desktop a bonus
- If it takes more than 2 taps it is wrong
- AI is the entry point, not a reward at the end of a long flow
- Compliance (SEF/SIBA) is a superpower, not a chore
- Warm palette: like sun on a white Portuguese wall
- One accent only (terracotta), max 10% surface

## Tone
Practical. Confident. Warm but never cute. Portuguese-first. No em dashes. No ellipsis as personality. Short sentences.
